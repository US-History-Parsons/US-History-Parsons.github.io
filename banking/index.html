Impact of the Great Depression on Banking



    Banks Fail

    Thousands of banks failed because people rushed to withdraw their money, and banks didn’t have enough cash to cover all deposits.

    ↓

    Economy Shrinks

    When banks failed, lending stopped, reducing spending and investment, and causing the economy to shrink even more.

    ↓

    Mass Poverty

    Many people lost their life savings, couldn’t access money, lost their jobs, and struggled to afford basic necessities, making everyday life much harder during the Great Depression.
